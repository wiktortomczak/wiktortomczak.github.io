Instructions

To generate balanced teams, with team-average player ratings as close as possible:

  1. Add players to the game
    ( button in the players panel left).
  2. Fill teams automatically
    ( buttons in the teams panel top right).
    and optionally
  3. Balance teams automatically
    ( button in the teams panel top right).

Manual adjustments are possible at any step:

  • Seed / partially prefill teams with selected players.
  • Automatically fill (complete) partially filled teams.
  • Move players between teams / back to unassigned.
  • Add more players / remove players from the game.
  • Restart / change number of teams.
All steps can be done any number of times in any order.

In addition, it is possible to:

  • Mark as captain one player per team .
  • Pin players to the team, so that they are not moved when auto-balancing teams.

Fill / balance algorithms

Fill teams randomly

Unassigned players are chosen at random, one at a time, and added to the team with the lowest number of players (to one of such teams chosen at random if there is more than one).

Fill teams strongest first

Unassigned players are chosen in order of rating (highest rating first) and added to the team with the lowest number of players and the lowest average rating (first such team if there is more than one).

Balance teams

Pairs of players from different teams are chosen at random and swapped if it gives more balanced teams, as measured by balance score: team-average rating range. Pinned players are not swapped. Balancing runs for a limited number of swaps.
